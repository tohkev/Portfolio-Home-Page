A Bit About Me

Hello! I'm Kevin Toh, and I am an aspiring web developer learning the ins and outs of programming through sites like Udemy and from my local community college (shoutout to you PCC!). I have a Bachelor's in Business Administration, Finance from the University of California, Riverside and am trying to transition into a technical role as a web developer.

Above all else, I enjoy creating! Programming gives me that outlet to create and implement ideas that others may find entertaining with just a few taps on the keyboard. My free time is spent on learning new concepts, working on projects, working out at the gym, and traveling! If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to reach out!
